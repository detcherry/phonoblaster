Phonoblaster

"Phonoblaster gives artists, record labels and music bloggers the ability to broadcast their favorite music directly to their fans!"

You create your station
You play your favorite music
And let the world listen to it!

Built on top of their Facebook pages, our application enables artists, music bloggers, even brands, engage their community and drive more traffic to their page with music.

Of course, it also helps fans connect in real time with their favorite artists: they do not only discover new music or their influences but can also chat with them and suggest other cool tracks to broadcast during the live set.

The experience is all the more social that friends of fans can easily tuned into a station thanks to the stories published in the Facebook ticker.

Right now, music comes primarily from the Youtube API but Phonoblaster plans to add more services such as Soundcloud in the next couple weeks.

Initially, we started working on this idea because we thought the most relevant music recommendations come from someone you “like”. That’s why we decided to tap into the less known part of the Facebook social graph: the connections between users and pages.

Currently in beta, the product has attracted hundreds of artists, music bloggers or record labels. From all over the world, these broadcasters have started programming radio-like shows, making contests or dj-sets.

Based in Paris, we are in the process of raising money among business angels or acceleration programs.
